About Us

हॉटेल जगदंबा विषय

Founded in 2014, Hotel Jagdamba has grown from a small hotel to a beloved chain of 8 restaurants across Maharashtra. Our roots remain firmly planted in the rural traditions of Maharashtra's culinary heritage.

आमच्या राशीन गावातील पाककृती आणि पारंपारिक पद्धतीने बनवलेल्या जेवणाचा अनुभव घ्या. प्रत्येक थाळीत आमच्या गावाची आणि आमच्या पिढ्यान् पिढ्यांच्या पाककृतीची ओळख आहे.

What started as a humble venture to share authentic Maharashtrian non-vegetarian cuisine has now become a destination for food lovers across the state. Our signature "Rashinchi Special Mutton Thali" has won hearts with its rustic flavors and generous portions.
